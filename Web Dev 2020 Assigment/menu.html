Meals and Desserts

freshly made every day

We take pride in creating an exemplary customer experience. Our food is prepared daily using fresh locally sourced ingredients.

Our Specialty Cakes

We take pride in our specialist cakes. They are freshly baked each morning hours before we open. Allowing even our earliest customers to have a sweet morning treat to start their day. We have hired a seasoned cake baker and pastry chef to be part of our team allowing us to provide delicious desserts with your cup of java.

We have a wide variety of cakes for our customers.

We have cakes for customers partaking in vegan and vegetarian diets. Using natural diary and egg alternatives to create cakes that are just as good as the real thing.

Our traditional cakes are made with the best animal and plant based ingredients which are locally sourced from Irish farms.

Our cakes and pastries are subjectively our best desserts, if you visit you really can't afford to miss them.

Sandwitches and Wraps

We offer a wide variety of sandwitches and wraps. Unlike most café's we hand prepare them for you just like a deli. This provides a much more fresh and delicious snack with your afternoon coffee.

Soup

Fresh and hot soup available with fresh bread buns at noon each day until 2pm.
